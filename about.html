About me

I am Abdullah Kadiwala, and I am a student looking forward to becoming a computer programmer and web designer in the future. I was born in Mumbai, India and then moved to Johannesburg, South Africa.

I did my primary Schooling in South Africa where I was able to learn many skills. I developed my interest in the field of computers since a very young age. I used to try different styles and designs while completing my project given in school and should always try to do new things with the computers. Along with computers I was also interested in playing cricket and Soccer.

I spent 10 years in South Africa which has given me many great memories. I am a very big fan of Cricket and Soccer, and I was lucky to have watched many matches at the Stadiums in South Africa which include matches of the ICC World Cup , FIFA World Cup , ICC Champions Trophy and many other.

In 2013, we left South Africa and moved to Vadodara, India. It felt quite different at the start but then everything got back on track. I completed my Secondary and Higher Secondary Education and with time my interests in the field of computer grew as I was able to learn different languages and skills. I learned BASIC, SQL and Python and I was able to make different projects with the help of these languages.

And now I am looking forward to coming Canada and get closer to my goal of becoming a computer engineer.
